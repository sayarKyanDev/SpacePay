To provide and improve our services, we collect various types of information, including:

We may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information such as:

  • Your name

When making payments for Starlink bills through the App, the transactions are processed by trusted third-party payment gateways (such as KBZ Pay, Wave Money). We do not collect or store your sensitive payment card details or banking credentials. Your payment data is securely transmitted to these providers, and their privacy policies apply.

We automatically collect information about your device and how you use the App ("Usage Data"). This may include:

  • Your device's IP address

This data helps us maintain and improve the App's functionality and user experience.

D. Google Sign-In Data

If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect basic profile information such as your Google account email and name to authenticate and personalize your experience.
